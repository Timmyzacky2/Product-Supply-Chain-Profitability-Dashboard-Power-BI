# Product-Supply-Chain-Profitability-Dashboard-Power-BI

### Dashboard Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides insights into product demand, inventory availability, supply shortages, profitability, and operational losses. The report helps businesses monitor supply chain performance and evaluate financial outcomes to support better inventory planning and decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to balance product demand with available inventory. Insufficient stock levels can result in supply shortages, lost sales opportunities, and reduced customer satisfaction. At the same time, poor inventory management can negatively impact profitability.

This dashboard was developed to help stakeholders:

* Monitor daily product demand trends
* Evaluate inventory availability
* Identify supply shortages
* Track profit and loss performance
* Improve inventory planning decisions
* Support operational efficiency

---

# 📊 Dashboard Features

## Page 1: Supply & Demand Analysis

This page focuses on inventory performance and supply chain efficiency.

### Key KPIs

#### Average Demand per Day

Measures the average quantity of products demanded daily.

**Business Value**

* Identifies customer demand patterns
* Supports inventory forecasting
* Helps optimize stock replenishment

#### Average Availability per Day

Measures the average quantity of products available daily.

**Business Value**

* Monitors inventory health
* Ensures adequate stock levels
* Supports operational planning

#### Total Supply Shortage

Measures the gap between product demand and available supply.

**Business Value**

* Highlights stock-out risks
* Identifies inventory bottlenecks
* Supports proactive inventory management

---

## Page 2: Profit & Loss Analysis

This page focuses on financial performance metrics.

### Key KPIs

#### Total Profit

Measures total profit generated from business operations.

**Business Value**

* Evaluates overall financial performance
* Supports revenue optimization strategies
* Tracks profitability trends

#### Total Loss

Measures total losses incurred during operations.

**Business Value**

* Identifies financial leakages
* Supports cost-reduction initiatives
* Improves operational efficiency

#### Average Loss per Day

Measures average daily losses.

**Business Value**

* Monitors ongoing financial risks
* Tracks operational performance
* Supports performance improvement initiatives
